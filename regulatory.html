Entity Characterization & Tax Status

Wyoming DAO Framework: LiteWater Capital DAO LLC operates under the Wyoming DAO LLC framework (WY Filing ID: 2026-001972207 / LEI: 984500360E6D6AG10C28). The structure is optimized for rapid blockchain settlement and explicitly delegates day-to-day settlement of digital assets to be conducted algorithmically under a cryptographic multi-signature consensus.

Disregarded Entity: LiteWater Capital DAO LLC is not an independently tax-exempt entity; it is a single-member, disregarded entity for federal tax purposes. Pursuant to Treasury Regulations § 301.7701-2(c)(2), a disregarded entity takes on the precise tax character of its owner. The LLC functions operationally as an internal treasury auxiliary entity, exclusively managing the capital of its parent Unincorporated Church and Spiritual Association, while fully inheriting the statutory tax immunities of its parent.

Tax Exclusion and Non-Reporting Mandate

  • 26 U.S.C. Code § 508(c)(1)(A) Mandatory Exception: The parent entity is statutorily exempt from applying for 501(c)(3) status and is automatically recognized as tax-exempt. As a disregarded subsidiary, LiteWater Capital inherits this mandatory exception.
  • 26 U.S.C. Code § 6033(a)(3)(A) Mandatory Exclusion: As an integrated auxiliary acting exclusively to manage funds for the parent, we are strictly prohibited from filing Form 990 (Annual Information Return). We are not required, nor permitted, to issue 1099s or similar tax reporting documentation for OTC conversion events.
  • SEC Family Office Exemption: The LLC acts exclusively as a private family office managing the internal treasury of its parent entity. It does not solicit or manage outside retail capital, strictly exempting it from registration under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 pursuant to 17 CFR § 275.202(a)(11)(G)-1.
  • FATCA Status: We operate as an Excepted Non-Financial Foreign Entity (NFFE) / Tax-Exempt Parent, as verified by federal forms.
  • FinCEN BOI Exemption: 31 U.S.C. § 5336(a)(11)(B)(xix) explicitly exempts any entity described in section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code. We qualify as a tax-exempt entity under the Corporate Transparency Act and are STRICTLY EXEMPT from FinCEN Beneficial Ownership Information reporting requirements.

Not Available to US Retail

The services, vaults, and tokens developed by LiteWater Capital DAO LLC (including JAB01) are not available to US retail investors. LiteWater operates strictly as a private fund manager for the treasury of its parent organization and does not solicit capital from the general public.
